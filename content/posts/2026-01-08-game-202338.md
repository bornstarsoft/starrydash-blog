The newly launched game AiliA offers a captivating experience that combines mirror-based mechanics with intricate puzzle-solving. Developed by IceToad Studio LLC and published by Gamirror Games, AiliA invites players to step into the shoes of Aria, a young girl navigating through a beautifully crafted world where reality and reflection intertwine. With over 100 puzzles spread across seven distinct themed areas, players are challenged to manipulate mirrors not just as reflections but as interactive elements that change their environment.

AiliA stands out with its unique gameplay that incorporates water reflections and recursive mirror structures, pushing the boundaries of traditional puzzle games. The game also features a special collaboration bundle with Moncage, offering fans an additional discount, making it an attractive option for puzzle enthusiasts. With a free demo available on Steam, players can dive into this enchanting world and uncover the story hidden within the memories of sisters Aria and Lia.

As the gaming community embraces innovative mechanics, AiliA is a testament to the creativity that can emerge from simple concepts like mirrors. Will you take on the challenge and explore the depths of this reflective adventure?
