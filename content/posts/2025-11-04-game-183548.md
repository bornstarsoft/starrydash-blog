In an exciting turn of events for Nintendo fans, both Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza have been shortlisted for the prestigious 'Console Game of the Year' at the Golden Joystick Awards. This recognition not only highlights the impressive launch lineup for the Switch 2 but also showcases the enduring appeal of these iconic franchises. With the gaming community buzzing, it's clear that these titles have made a significant impact in the competitive landscape of 2025.

The nominations come as no surprise, given the innovative gameplay and engaging experiences both games offer. Mario Kart World is vying for 'Best Multiplayer', while Donkey Kong Bananza is recognized for its 'Best Audio Design'. As the awards approach, fans are eager to see how these beloved titles will fare against formidable competitors like Death Stranding 2 and Ghost of Ytei.

As we look forward to the announcement of the winners on November 21, 2025, one can't help but wonder: will Nintendo's latest entries secure their place in gaming history? The excitement is palpable, and the voting is now open for fans to support their favorites!
