Larian Studios has officially announced a new game in the beloved Divinity series, reigniting excitement among RPG fans worldwide. This new installment, simply titled Divinity for now, marks a return to the franchise that put Larian on the map before their recent success with Baldurs Gate 3. The announcement also clarifies the mystery surrounding the ominous statue known as the Hellstone, which had gamers speculating about its significance for weeks.

The game promises to deliver an RPG experience with unprecedented depth and breadth, as indicated by its intriguing tagline: The gods are silent. Rivellon bleeds. New powers stir. With Larian's track record of crafting immersive worlds and engaging narratives, fans are eager to see how this new title will expand upon the rich lore of the Divinity universe.

As Larian shifts its focus from the Dungeons & Dragons universe to explore its own intellectual properties, the gaming community is left wondering: what new adventures and challenges await us in Rivellon? The anticipation is palpable, and we cant wait to learn more about this exciting new chapter in the Divinity saga.
