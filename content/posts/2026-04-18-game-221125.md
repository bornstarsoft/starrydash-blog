PEARL ABYSS has made history with its open-world action-adventure game, 'Crimson Desert', surpassing 5 million copies sold globally in just 26 days. This remarkable achievement marks a significant milestone as it is the fastest sales pace in Korean console game history, outpacing even last year's Game of the Year winner, 'Clair Obscur: Expedition 33', which took five months to reach the same figure.

The company expressed gratitude to its community, referring to players as 'Greymanes' who have explored the vast world of Pywel and contributed to the game's success. This rapid sales growth not only highlights the game's popularity but also sets a new benchmark for future titles in the Korean gaming industry. As we look ahead, it will be interesting to see how 'Crimson Desert' continues to evolve and what new heights it can achieve in the gaming landscape.
