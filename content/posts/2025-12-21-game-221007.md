SEGA is set to celebrate the half-anniversary of Persona5: The Phantom X with an exciting update that introduces beloved characters Justine and Caroline from the original Persona 5. This update, scheduled for December 25, marks a significant milestone for the free-to-play gacha game, which has been captivating players with its unique take on the Persona universe.

The addition of Justine and Caroline not only expands the roster of playable characters but also enriches the gameplay experience for fans who have followed the franchise. As attendants of the Velvet Room, these characters bring their own flair and abilities, making them valuable assets in the game. This update is a testament to SEGA's commitment to keeping the game fresh and engaging for both new and returning players.
