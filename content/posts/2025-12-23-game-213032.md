Bloober Team, known for its work on titles like Silent Hill 2 and Layers of Fear, has recently teased its upcoming Nintendo exclusive, Project M. According to CEO Piotr Babieno, this game aims to captivate fans of iconic horror franchises such as Resident Evil, Silent Hill, and Eternal Darkness. He hinted that Project M will introduce a unique gameplay experience that could only be realized on Nintendo hardware, promising a bold twist that sets it apart from traditional horror games.

Babieno expressed his excitement about the project, stating that the team is collaborating with top-tier game creators to ensure that Project M exceeds expectations. He reminisced about his passion for Nintendo, revealing that Bloober was founded to fulfill his dream of creating games for the platform. With such high aspirations and a commitment to innovation, fans are eagerly awaiting more details about this mysterious title.
