Game Pass has just welcomed a new gem to its library: the critically acclaimed prison survival RPG, Back to the Dawn. With an overwhelmingly positive rating on Steam, this pixel art role-playing game invites players to step into the shoes of Thomas the Fox, an investigative journalist wrongfully imprisoned for uncovering government secrets. The game not only challenges players to survive in the brutal confines of Boulderton Prison but also to forge alliances and plan their escape before time runs out.

Launched on July 18, Back to the Dawn is available on Game Pass for PC, Xbox, and Cloud, making it accessible to a wide range of players. For those looking to purchase it outright, the game is priced at $19.99 USD, with a limited-time launch discount bringing it down to $16.99 USD for the first week. This title is sure to keep players on the edge of their seats as they navigate through its gripping narrative and pixelated world.

With other exciting titles like RoboCop: Rogue City and Wuchang: Fallen Feathers joining Game Pass soon, it's an exciting time for gamers. Will you be diving into Back to the Dawn, or are you looking forward to the next big release?
