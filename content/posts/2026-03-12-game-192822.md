The beloved PS1 shoot 'em up, Sonic Wings Special, is making a triumphant return to modern consoles this week, and retro gaming enthusiasts couldn't be more excited. This re-release is part of Hamster Corporation's Console Archives series, which aims to revive classic titles that have been lost to time, offering a nostalgic experience for both new players and those who fondly remember the original.

Originally launched in 1996, Sonic Wings Special combines elements from its predecessors and features an impressive lineup of 26 fighter ships. With 17 stages and a unique branching path system, players can enjoy a fresh experience with each playthrough. This re-release will be based on the hard-to-find PS1 version, making it a must-have for collectors and fans alike.

As we look forward to the re-release on PS5 and Switch 2, priced at just $11.99, it raises the question: which classic game do you hope to see revived next? Retro gaming is clearly making a comeback, and the future looks bright for fans of vintage titles.
