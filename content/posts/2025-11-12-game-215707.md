Sony's recent State of Play presentation focused on the vibrant gaming scene in Japan and Asia, unveiling a total of 28 exciting announcements. From the highly anticipated remake of Dragon Quest VII to the return of the beloved Tokyo Xtreme Racer series, this showcase highlighted the diverse range of titles that are set to captivate gamers in the coming years. Notably, the event also introduced new DLCs for popular franchises like Gran Turismo 7 and Monster Hunter Wilds, ensuring that fans have plenty to look forward to.

Among the standout reveals was the announcement of a new PS5 Digital Japanese Edition, which aims to make the console more accessible to local gamers. Priced at 55,000, this version reflects Sony's commitment to catering to its home market while also introducing innovative gaming experiences like the upcoming Elden Ring Nightreign DLC.
