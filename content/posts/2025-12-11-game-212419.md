This December, PlayStation Plus subscribers have a lot to look forward to with the addition of major titles like **Assassins Creed Mirage** and **Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty** to the Game Catalog. These games are set to enhance the gaming experience for both Extra and Premium tier members, showcasing Sony's commitment to providing high-quality content to its user base.

Alongside these blockbuster titles, players can also dive into the newly released **Skate Story**, as well as family-friendly options like **Paw Patrol: Grand Prix** and **Lego Horizon Adventures**. Notably, the classic fighter **Soulcalibur III** makes its way to the Premium Classic Catalog, appealing to nostalgia-driven gamers. This diverse lineup ensures that theres something for everyone this month.
