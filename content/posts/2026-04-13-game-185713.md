The highly anticipated reboot of Fable is facing internal delays, primarily due to concerns over the release timing with Grand Theft Auto 6, which is set to launch in November. According to industry insider Jeff Grubb, Microsoft is wary of releasing Fable too close to such a blockbuster title, which could overshadow its launch. This has led to speculation that Fable's release could slip into 2027, although there are still hopes for a late 2026 debut.

Fable was fully revealed just a couple of months ago, and its developers at Playground Games have expressed their commitment to honoring the legacy of the original Lionhead Studios. With the gaming calendar looking sparse in late 2026, the decision to delay could be strategic, allowing Fable to carve out its own space in the market. As the gaming community eagerly awaits more news, one has to wonder: will Fable manage to stand out in a year dominated by the likes of GTA 6?
