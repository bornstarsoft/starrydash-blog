If you're a fan of puzzle games but often find them too challenging, then Botany Manor might just be the perfect fit for you. Currently available for free on the Epic Games Store, this cozy first-person puzzle game invites players to step into the shoes of botanist Arabella Greene, exploring a Victorian manor to complete her botanical research. Unlike many other puzzle games that can be frustratingly difficult, Botany Manor strikes a balance between engaging gameplay and accessibility, making it ideal for casual gamers.

The developers have crafted a peaceful setting where the puzzles are designed to be intriguing without overwhelming players. Laure De May, the lead developer, emphasizes that the challenge lies in the number of clues and steps involved rather than the complexity of the puzzles themselves. This approach has resonated with players, as reflected in the positive reviews on Steam, where many appreciate the games cozy atmosphere and manageable difficulty.

As we dive deeper into the world of gaming, it's refreshing to see titles like Botany Manor that cater to a broader audience, allowing everyone to enjoy the thrill of puzzle-solving without the stress.
