In the realm of indie gaming, few genres capture the imagination quite like steampunk horror, and Game Pop Studio's upcoming title, *They Will Come*, promises to deliver an unforgettable experience. Set aboard a perilous airship in 1897, players will navigate a world filled with Wretched Ones and towering automatons, all while solving intricate puzzles and engaging in stealth gameplay. The unique blend of immersive visuals and a bittersweet narrative sets the stage for a gripping adventure that is sure to resonate with fans of the genre.

Players will step into the shoes of Benjamin, a young hero armed with a trusty slingshot and accompanied by his giant robot companion, Talus. As they traverse the eerie corridors of the Endeavor, the tension mounts with every shadowy encounter. The game not only challenges players' puzzle-solving skills but also immerses them in a rich lore that reimagines history through a steampunk lens. With breathtaking graphics crafted by industry veteran Jeff Troutman, the game's environment promises to be as captivating as the story itself.

As we look toward its anticipated release in 2026, *They Will Come* stands as a testament to the creativity and innovation that indie developers bring to the gaming landscape. Will this game redefine the steampunk horror genre? Only time will tell, but one thing is for sure: the journey aboard the Endeavor is bound to be a thrilling ride.
