Get ready for an exciting new gaming experience as "Good Luck," a physics-based urban survival game, is set to launch on April 8 for PC, with console versions for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S to follow. This game promises to deliver an exhilarating mix of platforming and survival challenges, where players must navigate through a city filled with bizarre traps and hazards. The unique premise of racing to a meeting while overcoming absurd obstacles is sure to captivate gamers looking for something fresh and entertaining.

Developed by the talented independent studio Happybara Games and published by Ultimate Games S.A., "Good Luck" offers both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players. This feature allows friends to team up and tackle the chaotic urban landscape together, enhancing the fun and replayability of the game. As we anticipate its release, it's clear that this title could become a favorite among fans of co-op gaming.

With the rise of cooperative gameplay and the demand for innovative titles, "Good Luck" stands out as a promising addition to the genre. Will it meet the expectations of the gaming community? Only time will tell, but the excitement is palpable as we count down to launch day!
