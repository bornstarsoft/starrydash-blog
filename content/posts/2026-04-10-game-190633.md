In a shocking revelation, Gunzilla Games, the blockchain game development studio co-founded by acclaimed director Neill Blomkamp, is facing serious allegations from former employees regarding unpaid wages. Reports indicate that some staff members have not received their salaries for several months, raising concerns about the company's financial practices and treatment of its workforce.

Former animator Paul Creamer took to LinkedIn to share his experience, claiming he hasnt been paid since October 2025. He highlighted that many of his colleagues are in similar situations, with some waiting even longer for their dues. Despite assurances from upper management about resolving payment delays, the situation has not improved, leading to frustration and feelings of exploitation among the staff.

This situation serves as a stark reminder of the challenges facing employees in the gaming industry, especially in emerging sectors like blockchain gaming. As the industry evolves, its crucial for workers to advocate for their rights and ensure that their contributions are valued and compensated fairly. How can the gaming community come together to support those affected by such predatory practices?
