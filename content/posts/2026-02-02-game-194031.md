PlayStation Plus users are in for a treat with the addition of a unique indie title, The Exit 8. This free puzzle game, recently introduced to the PS Plus Extra and Premium tiers, offers a chilling yet captivating experience that stands out among the nine new games added this January. With its intriguing mechanics and eerie atmosphere, The Exit 8 has quickly garnered attention from players looking for something different.

Developed by Kotake Create, The Exit 8 immerses players in a first-person journey through a haunting Japanese metro tunnel. As you navigate this unsettling environment, your task is to identify various anomalies that can range from harmless discrepancies to spine-chilling visuals. The challenge lies in recognizing these anomalies and reacting swiftly to progress through the games looping structure. With a completion time of just a couple of hours, its an engaging experience that doesnt overstay its welcome.

Players have praised the game for its blend of obvious and subtle anomalies, making for a fun yet unnerving gameplay experience. While some may find it too short, the thrill of uncovering the mysteries within The Exit 8 keeps players coming back for more. As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, its exciting to see indie titles like this making waves on platforms like PlayStation Plus. Will you take the plunge into this creepy puzzle adventure?
