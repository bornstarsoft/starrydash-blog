Gamirror Games is set to launch AiliA, a visually stunning mirror-based puzzle game, on January 9, 2026. This unique title invites players into a mesmerizing 3D world where they will navigate through reflections and manipulate mirrors to solve intricate puzzles. As Aria, players will embark on a heartfelt journey to rescue her twin sister trapped in a mirrored realm, blending emotional storytelling with engaging gameplay.

AiliA stands out not just for its captivating narrative but also for its innovative mechanics. Players will find themselves immersed in a fully tangible mirrored environment, where every reflection holds potential pathways and challenges. With over 100 puzzles across seven beautifully crafted stages inspired by Eastern aesthetics, AiliA promises to deliver an experience that combines spatial awareness with creative problem-solving. 

As the release date approaches, gamers can get a taste of the adventure with a playable demo available on Steam. Will you be ready to explore the depths of AiliA's mirrored world and uncover the secrets hidden within?
